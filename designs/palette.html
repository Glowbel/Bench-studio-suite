(Section 9)

The Merger Zone

Where Bench meets Filigree. The current Bench app has unique, technically-correct UI — the capsule, the tab-bar, the node orbs, the skill cards. Here we sit those elements inside the new design language and ask: what survives, what adapts, where does grace replace machinery?

Bench's bones are good. Its colors lean cyan where Filigree leans verdant. Its mono-font labels feel technical where Cormorant + Outfit feel inhabited. Its hairline borders are correct but unbezelled — surfaces float instead of being set. Each subsection below shows a Bench element with Filigree applied at the surface. The structure is unchanged. Only the vocabulary shifts.
